About Our School

Discover our rich heritage, mission, and the impact we're making in girls' education in Kenya

Our History

Founded in 2011, Mother of Mercy Girls Secondary School has been at the forefront of girls' education in Kenya's Rift Valley for over a decade. Our mission is to provide holistic education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit.

We believe in empowering young women to become leaders in their communities through academic excellence, moral integrity, and compassionate service.

Our campus provides a safe, nurturing environment where girls can explore their potential, develop their talents, and build lifelong friendships.

"Education is the great equalizer for our girls and will help them transform our society for progress in so many fields."

- Sr. Rose, Founder

Our Mission

To enlighten, Inspire and Empower a girl child in their Community through a rich variety of activities in becoming a holistic individual purposed to face challenges of a dynamic global village

Our Vision

A place where girls are challenged both mind, body and spirit to be who they are in order to reach their potentials

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and moral principles in all our actions, fostering trust and accountability within our school community.

Professionalism

We maintain high standards of behavior, discipline, and ethics in all we do, ensuring that every task is carried out with dedication and competence.

Hard Work

We believe in diligence and perseverance as keys to success, motivating every student to give their best effort in academics and life pursuits.

Teamwork

We value collaboration and unity, working together towards common goals and supporting one another to achieve collective success.

Service

We nurture a spirit of compassion and social responsibility, encouraging our students to use their knowledge and skills to positively impact society.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, character, and personal development, inspiring every girl to realize her full potential.

Our Facilities

Dormitories

Modern Dormitories

Completed in 2017-2018, our dormitories now house more than 350 students with modern showers and baths, creating a comfortable home away from home.

  • Safe, secure housing with 24/7 supervision
  • Modern bathroom facilities with hot water
  • Study areas and common rooms for socializing

Science Labs

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped laboratories for chemistry, biology, physics, and agriculture completed in 2019, providing hands-on learning experiences.

  • Modern equipment and safety gear
  • Safety-first design with proper ventilation
  • Hands-on learning with real experiments

Sports Facilities

Sports Facilities

A beautiful track and field completed in 2024, supporting all physical education programs and athletic competitions.

  • Olympic-quality running track
  • Soccer field for team sports
  • Basketball and volleyball courts
