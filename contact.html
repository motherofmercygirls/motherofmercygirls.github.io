Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our school

What are the admission requirements?

Students must have completed primary education, pass our entrance exam, and provide necessary documentation including birth certificate and previous school reports.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships for qualified students. Applications are reviewed annually.

What are the school terms?

We follow the Kenyan academic calendar with three terms: January-April, May-August, and September-November.

Is boarding available?

Yes, we have modern boarding facilities that can accommodate over 350 students with 24/7 supervision and care.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer sports, music, drama, clubs, community service, and various leadership programs to ensure holistic development.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

You can schedule a tour by calling our admissions office or using the "Schedule a Visit" button on this page.
