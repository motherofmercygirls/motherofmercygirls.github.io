Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • Completed primary school education with strong academic record
  • Passing score on our entrance examination in English, Mathematics, and Science
  • Recommendation letter from previous school headteacher
  • Original birth certificate or affidavit

Application Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (2)
  • Copy of primary school leaving certificate
  • Medical report from recognized health facility

Tuition & Financial Aid

Scholarship Opportunities

We believe financial circumstances should not prevent qualified students from accessing quality education. Through the Mercy Center Foundation, USA, we offer partial scholarships to students with demonstrated financial need.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Strong academic performance
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Commitment to community service

Application Process

  • Complete scholarship application form
  • Submit supporting financial documents
  • Interview with scholarship committee
